This site works as a personal homepage, portfolio, and resume landing page.

I do not want it to be a page that only lists what I know. Instead, it is built as a long-term content system where work, writing, and growth can be presented in a clear and credible way.

At the moment, it mainly serves three roles:

- **Portfolio**: showcase projects that are worth presenting repeatedly
- **Blog**: publish technical notes, learning records, and reflections
- **Resume Hub**: create a reliable first impression for hiring and collaboration

Over time, I will continue replacing sample content with real projects, better skill detail, clearer contact information, and more complete career experience.
