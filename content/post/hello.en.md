This is a sample learning note.

The goal is to move the site from being simply “a blog that works” to “a personal portfolio site that supports long-term growth and career presentation.”

The first round focused on a few essential tasks:

- rebuilding site configuration and fixing deployment
- shifting the visual direction toward a cleaner and more professional tone
- adding portfolio, skills, learning journey, and contact pages
- making the site suitable for both writing and project presentation

Over time, real projects, experiments, and learning notes will continue to replace the sample content.
